# Part 5 – AI Reflection and Prompt Engineering

## 1. AI Tools Used

- ChatGPT (primary tool for research, explanations, and CRM system design)

---

## 2. Prompts Used

### Prompt 1 – CRM Overview
What is a CRM system and what are its main purposes in business?

**Why it worked well:**
It provided a clear definition and explained CRM in simple structured terms.

---

### Prompt 2 – CRM Modules
List the main modules of a CRM system and explain each one briefly.

**Why it worked well:**
It generated a structured breakdown of CRM components such as contacts, leads, opportunities, and reports.

---

### Prompt 3 – Database Design
What tables are needed for a CRM system using MySQL?

**Why it worked well:**
It provided a clear relational database structure commonly used in CRM systems.

---

### Prompt 4 – CRM Architecture
Design a simple CRM architecture using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL.

**Why it worked well:**
It produced a clear 3-tier architecture showing frontend, backend, and database layers.

---

### Prompt 5 – Security in CRM Systems
What security risks exist in CRM systems and how can they be prevented?

**Why it worked well:**
It explained key security topics such as authentication, SQL injection, XSS, and data protection.

---

## 3. AI Effectiveness Analysis

### What AI did well
- Explained technical concepts clearly
- Helped structure the assignment
- Generated diagrams
- well organized format 

---

### What AI struggled with
- Sometimes gave general answers instead of detailed system-specific solutions
- Required human verification for accuracy

---

### What required validation
- CRM history and evolution
- Industry comparisons
- Open-source CRM evaluation

---

### What surprised me
- How fast AI can generate structured technical reports
- generate images with better quality 

---

### What I would do differently next time
- Ask follow-up questions instead of one-time prompts

---

### Would I trust AI for software research?

AI is useful for learning and research, but it should not be fully trusted without verification.

---

## 4. Conclusion

AI helped significantly in understanding CRM systems, designing architecture, and organizing technical documentation. However, human review is still necessary to ensure accuracy and quality.
